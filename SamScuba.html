Scuba Trips

Little Cayman

In 2017, I went to Little Cayman. I went on six dives over three days. It was exclucively reef and wall diving

Dive Site Access

I dove on the south side of the island. Sites were accessed via boats that were docked at a pier. Dive site availability is heavily influinced by weather. Durring my trip, the north side of the island was to dangerous to dive due to the wind direction.

Rating

Overall, I would give this location a nine out of ten. The visibility was excellent and the water temperature was a constant 81 degrees.
